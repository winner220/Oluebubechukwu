Tomato Stew recipe

Tomato Stew is a simple and delicious meal that can be eaten with rice, yam or bread. It is easy to prepare and uses common ingredient.

Ingredient

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • pepper
  • salt
  • chicken,beef or fish
  • onion
  • vegetable oil
  • seasoning cube

Recipe

  1. Wash and blend the tomato, pepper and onions
  2. Boil the mixtureto reduce the excess water
  3. Heat the oil in the pot
  4. Add onion and fry for a few minutes
  5. add tomato paste and fry for a few minutes
  6. pour in the mixture and stir well
  7. Add cooked chicken, beef or fish
  8. Add salt and seasoning
  9. cook for a few minutes till its thick
